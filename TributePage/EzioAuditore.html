Ezio Auditore da Firenze

Ezio Auditore da Firenze, shown in his youth (left), adulthood (center) and old age (right)

A timeline of Ezio Auditore da Firenze's life:

    Events of Assassin's Creed II
  • 1459 Born in Firenze, Italy
  • 1476 Witnessed execution of his father Giovanni, older brother Federico, and younger brother Petruccio. Discovered his Assassin heritage.
  • 1476-1480 Assassinated members of the Pazzi Conspiracy in and around Firenze.
  • 1481 Sailed to Venezia with Leonardo da Vinci.
  • 1485-1488 Assassinated members of the Venetian Conspiracy. Stole the Apple of Eden, and was formally inducted into the Assassin Order.
  • 1497 Assassinated Girolamo Savonarola and his nine lieutenants, overthrowing his rule of Firenze.
  • 1499 Defeated Pope Alexander VI, and received a message from the Precursor Minerva, meant for his descendant Desmond Miles.

    • Events of Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood
  • 1500 Was severely wounded during the destruction of Monteriggioni. Joined Niccolò Machiavelli in Rome to fight the Borgia.
  • 1500-1503 Liberated Rome from the Borgia. Was promoted to Mentor of the Italian Brotherhood of Assassins.
  • 1506 Locked his Apple of Eden in the First Civilization vault beneath the Colosseum. Travelled to Valencia with Niccolò Machiavelli and Leonardo da Vinci.
  • 1507 Assassinated Cesare Borgia.

    • Events of Assassin's Creed: Revelations
  • 1510 Travelled to Masyaf to find Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad's library.
  • 1511 Was captured by Byzantine Templars, but escaped execution.
  • 1512 Unlocked Altaïr's library, but left the Apple of Eden locked inside. Witnessed another Precursor message alongside Desmond Miles.
  • 1513 Retired as Mentor of the Italian Brotherhood of Assassins. Became the father of Flavia Auditore.
  • 1514 Became the father of Marcello Auditore.
  • 1524 Died in Firenze, Italy, at the age of 65.

“My story is one of many thousands, and the world will not suffer if it ends too soon.”
