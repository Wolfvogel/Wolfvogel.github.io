Escape Room

One evening, walking back home from a party, you decide to take a shortcut through the park to get out of the rain quicker. Suddenly the ground beneath you moves, and crumbles. You try to scramble to the side, but the earth gives way and you start falling and sliding down a dark hole. Once you've come to a halt, you wipe some of the dust out of your eyes, and fumble around in your pocket for your phone. As you turn on the flashlight function, you notice that your legs are quickly becoming wet. You look around, and see that the cave-in must have opened up access to an underground body of water. You find yourself in what looks like an cavern, and water is rapidly flowing in. No time to call for help! You see two ways out. To your right there is an opening covered in moss, leading down; or you could try swimming in against the stream to where the water is coming from.
